Wing Classification

  • Aspect - ratio of width to chord
    1. High- Less Lift, Less Drag, Fast Run Rate
    2. Medium
    3. Low - More Lift, More Drag, Slower Run Rate
  • Surface Area Size
    1. Larger Areas produce more lift with less speed and travel slower
    2. Smaller Areas require more speed to generate lift but travel faster in flight
  • Mounting Styles
    1. Tuttle
    2. Track
    3. Bolted Mount
